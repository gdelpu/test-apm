SURVEY ON HANDLING QUALITY REQUIREMENTS
 IN AGILE FRAMEWORKS (01/2023)*
After the publication of Rainer Grau's RE Magazine article
on the topic of quality requirements, we called on our
community to participate in our survey on this topic. We
wanted to know how you assess the importance of quality
requirements in agile frameworks and whether you know
and use the concepts in your daily work. The results show
that although most respondents do not yet use any of the
concepts, they consider the topic relevant to their work.
*26 people participated in this survey.
