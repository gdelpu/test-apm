# Principles

Core delivery principles for the AI SDLC Foundation.

- Keep delivery reviewable and incremental.
- Make assumptions explicit — document what is known and what is guessed.
- Optimize for maintainability and delivery safety over velocity.
- Prefer clear boundaries over clever abstractions.
- Security and quality are non-negotiable — bake them in, don't bolt them on.
- Test-first development: tests document intent and prevent regressions.
- Observable by default: instrument critical flows from day one.
- Accessibility is a first-class requirement, not an afterthought.
